Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to professionally display my art. This site will have one page for my traditional art, and another for my digital art. My goal is to show my style, but also versatility throughout the website

Audience

This would be for prospective customers to see my work. They can look at my work in different forms including the website itself.
